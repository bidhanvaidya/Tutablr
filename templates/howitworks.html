What Is TuTablr

  Tutablr is a system to help manage your free time, contact other users, and to find and rate qualified tutors. Getting extra help for your subjects is made all the easier with a distance aware search function and tutors sorted by user reviews.


Using TuTablr

  Using TuTablr is simple to use! The TuTablr timetable is an easy, intuitive application that takes no time at all. Want to extend your free time? Simply drag your time units down for more time in the same day, or to the right to extend to more days. You can also tick the checkbox at the top of a day to block off any time for that day, with respect to class times. You will not be able to move or overwrite your university class times. They're important!


Tutors

  Tutors are what makes TuTablr so ingenious. You can search for a tutor for any of your available subjects by simply clicking the Search feature from the navigation bar and selecting the subject you need help in. Tutors are ordered by rating and can be searched by geographical distance, cost, and matching free time. Our tutors go through an aptitude test administered by qualified experts in each subject to determine if a tutor is indeed capable of tutoring a subject.
